    The best way to play Windows games on Mac.

    An open-source macOS game launcher with the ability to play Windows® games through a custom implementation of Apple's Game Porting Toolkit — supporting multiple platforms.

    for macOS 14+

    Friends gaming without you?
    Mythic can fix that.

    Time to throw all your virtual machines and subscriptions out of the window.

    Your library, your way.

    Easily manage all your games in one place, with a beautiful and customizable library.

    Some Windows® games may not be supported for use within Mythic.

    Open-source, open season!

    Something isn't as good as it should be? Dig through the source code and help make it better.

    Multiple launchers, one place.

    Install, set up, and launch all your games from an external launcher, or by yourself.

    Steam support is still in development.

    Import your own titles.

    Epic not enough? Import your own macOS or Windows® titles directly into Mythic, and the rest is history.

    Virtualization? Child's play.

    Create multiple environments of Windows® directly within Mythic, and keep all your games isolated safely from one another.

    Game management, without the fuss.

    Need to move your games around? Maybe, edit some settings? Add some flags? Mythic has you covered.

    We have even more to come, check out the roadmap. (Please note that Mythic is still in its alpha stage of development; expect bugs.)
